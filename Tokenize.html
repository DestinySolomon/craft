How to Tokenize or List Your Craft

Step 1: Sign Up & Connect Wallet

Create an account and securely connect your Web3 wallet to access tokenization tools.

Step 2: Upload Craft Details

Upload images, descriptions, materials, and origin of your handmade or digital product.

Step 3: Choose Token Type

Decide whether your craft will be minted as a unique NFT or a utility Token (e.g. for batches).

Step 4: Submit for Review

Our team verifies originality, content, and intent to ensure quality and prevent fraud.

Step 5: Approve & Mint

Once reviewed, you approve the final mint details and launch your token on-chain.

Step 6: Get Listed

Your tokenized craft is published to the marketplace and discoverable globally.

How It Works

Secure Tokenization

All crafts are minted on secure smart contracts with permanent metadata and anti-tamper protection.

Each token has a clear, traceable ownership history recorded on-chain for buyers’ trust.

Royalties & Rewards

You earn royalties on every secondary sale — powered by smart contract automation.

CraftiFi

Craft-based tokens and NFTs can move seamlessly across multiple blockchains. Unlocking wider exposure, access to global liquidity, and flexibility across ecosystems like Ethereum, Polygon, and more.
