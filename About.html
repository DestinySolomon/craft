Empowering Artisans. Tokenizing Craft.

CraftiFi bridges the gap between traditional craftsmanship and Web3 innovation, enabling artisans to tokenize their creations securely and globally.

Our Mission

At CraftiFi, our mission is to empower artisans by providing them with tools to convert their physical craftsmanship into secure, tradable digital assets. We aim to unlock new income streams, preserve culture, and bring real-world utility to the blockchain.

Our Story

CraftiFi was born from a desire to bring local craftsmanship into the digital era. After witnessing how many artisans struggled to reach modern markets, we envisioned a platform that would give them global access, ownership, and visibility through tokenization.

What We Offer

  • Tokenization of crafts as NFTs or ERC-20 tokens
  • Listing and discovery of unique, artisan-made items
  • Seamless integration with crypto wallets
  • $Craft token utility across the ecosystem
  • Security and authenticity checks before listing
